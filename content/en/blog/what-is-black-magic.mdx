## What Is Black Magic?

**Black magic** is the practice of using supernatural forces to cause harm, influence outcomes, or bend reality to one's will. For thousands of years, cultures across every continent have developed systems of dark magic — from the curse tablets of ancient Sumeria to the [Chinese Da Siu Yan tradition](/blog/what-is-da-siu-yan) that still thrives today.
