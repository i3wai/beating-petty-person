## Voodoo Magic and Curses

**Voodoo** is one of the most misunderstood spiritual traditions in the world. Far from the Hollywood image of pins in dolls, real Voodoo (Vodou) is a complex religion with roots in West Africa, developed in the Caribbean through the crucible of slavery. Its curse practices are real, structured, and deeply tied to [global traditions of magical retribution](/blog/how-to-curse-someone).
