## Hex Spells and Curses

**Hexing** is one of the oldest magical practices in the Western tradition. But what exactly is a hex — and how does it differ from a curse or a jinx? The distinction matters. A hex is targeted, deliberate, and personal. A curse is broader, often generational. A jinx is minor — bad luck rather than destruction.
